Biography

Hi, I'm Payden

I am a photographer and web developer in Omaha, Nebraska. I have been taking pictures since I was a child, but started more seriously about 3 years ago when I took classes at Metropolitan Community College. Growing up in Omaha, I have constantly been surrounded by art. Art studios, art galleries, art museums. In Omaha I have had unlimited access to the arts since I was young, and always took advantage of the great opportunities. Photography is a way that I am able to express myself when words are hard to use. I see the world in a different way than other people. I photograph things that most people would just overlook and never think could be art. I am in love with how much a photograph can hold. Photographs have so many different interpretations and feelings, and that is my favorite part of showing people my pictures. Everyone sees things in their own ways, and that's what makes every photograph unique. My goal is to help people realize that there is a lot they are missing and that sometimes you need to slow down and just look at what is around you.
